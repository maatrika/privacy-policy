Maatrika Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Maatrika ("we", "our", or "us") handles your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

  • Profile Information: Stored locally on your device only and not transmitted to our servers.
  • Purchase Information: In-app purchases are processed by third-party platforms such as Apple App Store and Google Play, as well as RevenueCat. We do not store your payment details.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect or store personal health data on our servers.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.
  • We do not display advertisements in the app.

Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to operate certain features of the app:

  • Apple App Store (for payments and subscriptions)
  • Google Play Store (for payments and subscriptions)
  • RevenueCat (for subscription management)

These services may process data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is primarily stored on your device. We take reasonable measures to ensure that your information is secure.

Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data you have provided
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Restrict or object to processing

Since most data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by uninstalling the app.

Children’s Privacy

Maatrika does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
